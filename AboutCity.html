Lets have a look

HYDERABAD

Hyderabad is a vibrant city where rich history meets modernity, highlighted by landmarks like the Charminar and Golconda Fort. Famous for its culinary delight, the city is particularly known for the world-renowned Hyderabadi Biryani.

WARANGAL

Once the capital of the Kakatiya dynasty, Warangal is rich in history, famous for the Warangal Fort and the Thousand Pillar Temple that reflect its architectural grandeur.

KHAIRATABAD

Khairatabad is a culturally rich neighborhood in Hyderabad, known for its historic temples. The Khairatabad Ganesh idol, renowned for its size, draws thousands of devotees each Ganesh Chaturthi.

ADILABAD

Adilabad is renowned for its rich tribal heritage and breathtaking natural beauty, celebrated through vibrant festivals like Sankranti, which feature traditional music and dance.

DANCE AND MUSIC

Telangana's cultural tapestry is enriched by the dynamic dance form of Perini Sivatandavam and vibrant folk music that together celebrate the region's rich heritage during festive occasions.

FESTIVALS

Telangana celebrates vibrant festivals like Bonalu, honoring goddess Mahakali, and Bathukamma, which showcases floral artistry during the harvest season.

FOOD

Telangana cuisine is distinguished by bold flavors, with specialties such as Hyderabadi Biryani, Sakinalu, and Pesarattu reflecting its agricultural roots.

HISTORICAL SPOTS AND FORTS

Golkonda Fort and the Qutb Shahi Tombs are key historical sites in Telangana, highlighting the region's architectural grandeur and historical significance.
